📅 Effective Date: 1 February 2026
🏢 Operator: PICK2WIN Pvt Ltd (UK)
⚖️ Jurisdiction: England & Wales
🎰 Regulator: UK Gambling Commission (UKGC)
🌍 Website: www.pick2win.uk
📨 Support: compliance@pick2win.uk

🎯 1. Purpose

The Know Your Customer (KYC) Policy ensures every PICK2WIN user is verified before withdrawing money to prevent identity fraud, financial crime, and underage access.
This policy follows:

UK Gambling Commission (UKGC) Remote Technical Standards
Money Laundering Regulations 2017 (MLR 2017)
FCA Payment Services Regulations
UK GDPR & Data Protection Act 2018

KYC ensures safety, fairness, and complete regulatory compliance.

🧭 2. Objectives

PICK2WINs KYC process ensures:

  1. Accurate verification of identity, age (18+), and UK residence
  2. Only legitimate UK users can withdraw funds
  3. Prevention of impersonation, underage access, and fraudulent activity
  4. Compliance with AML/CFT obligations
  5. Strong data protection and encryption under GDPR

🧾 3. Verification Partner SumSub

PICK2WIN uses SumSub (Sum & Substance Ltd), an FCA-registered, UKGC-compatible verification provider.

SumSub verifies:

🎂 Age check (must be 18+)
🪪 Identity authentication + biometric face match
🏠 Address verification
💳 Payment ownership matching

All processes are encrypted, audited, and compliant with UKGC & GDPR standards.

👤 4. KYC Verification Requirements

4.1 Acceptable Identity Proof (ONE of the following)

UK Passport
UK Driving Licence
National ID Card
Biometric Residence Permit

4.2 Acceptable Address Proof (ONE of the following)

Utility Bill (≤ 90 days)
Bank/Card Statement (≤ 90 days)
Council Tax Bill (current year)
Government-issued letter

4.3 Payment Ownership Verification

All deposits must come from an account/card in the same verified name
Business, joint, third-party payments are not permitted
Ownership mismatch results in immediate restriction

⭐ CRITICAL OPERATIONAL RULE

Users may deposit, subscribe, and play WITHOUT KYC, but:

❗ NO withdrawal is allowed until full KYC is completed and approved.

Why KYC is NOT forced at registration

Many users register but never become active → unnecessary verification cost
Reduces friction for new users while still compliant
Prevents verifying thousands of inactive accounts

Why withdrawal requires full KYC

Because money is leaving the platform and must go only to the verified real owner.
This prevents:

✔ Fraud
✔ Identity misuse
✔ Money laundering
✔ Third-party withdrawals

📌 User profile shows:
KYC Pending until approved
KYC Done after successful verification

💷 6. Incomplete or Failed KYC

If KYC fails or remains incomplete:

🚫 Withdrawals blocked
🚫 Payment ownership restricted
🚫 AML flags may freeze account temporarily
📩 Users receive instructions to resubmit acceptable documents
⏳ 30+ days of no action may lead to temporary suspension

🧩 7. Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)

EDD applies when:

Deposit/play behaviour exceeds affordability limits
AML triggers emerge (patterns, frequency, device mismatch)
Withdrawals appear inconsistent with declared income

EDD may require:

Payslips
Bank statements
Source of Funds (SoF)
Source of Wealth (SoW)

PICK2WIN rarely requests SoF/SoW because income-based monthly deposit limits already reduce AML risk.

⚙️ 8. Re-Verification Triggers

Re-verification occurs if:

User changes address or payment method
New device/IP anomalies detected
Inactivity for 6+ months
Requested during regulatory or AML audits

🔒 9. Data Protection & Retention

All KYC records encrypted with AES-256
Retained for minimum 5 years (legal obligation)
Access allowed only to authorised AML/KYC officers
Shared ONLY with regulatory authorities when required:

  • UKGC
  • FCA
  • NCA
  • Law enforcement

No KYC data is ever sold or used for non-compliance purposes.

🧠 10. User Rights (GDPR)

Users can request:

📄 Access to their KYC data
✏️ Correction of inaccurate information
🗑 Deletion (unless we must retain it legally)
🚫 Processing restriction (where permitted)

Requests → privacy@pick2win.uk
Response → within 30 days

 ❌ Third-party funding
 ❌ Device/IP manipulation
 ❌ Collusion or coordinated gameplay

Serious violations →
⛔ Immediate permanent ban + report to UKGC, FCA, NCA.
