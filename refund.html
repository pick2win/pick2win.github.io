🎯 1. Purpose

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains how PICK2WIN processes refunds, ensuring:

📘 Transparency
⚖️ User protection
🏛 Full compliance with UK law

Including compliance with:

  • UK Gambling Commission LCCP
  • FCA Payment Services Regulations
  • Consumer Rights Act 2015
  • Data Protection Act 2018

Refunds follow a strict wallet-based system no hidden deductions and no refunds to external bank accounts.

👥 2. Eligibility

Refunds apply only to:

✔ Users who completed full KYC
✔ Transactions conducted inside the official PICK2WIN app/website

Refunds are not available for:

❌ Unverified accounts
❌ Suspended/closed accounts
❌ Users flagged for AML or fraud
❌ Past chargebacks or payment disputes

💰 3. Non-Refundable Transactions

Once processed, these cannot be refunded:

✔ Successful deposits
 ✔ Contest entry fees for contests that launch
 ✔ Activated subscription payments
 ✔ Bonus credits or promotional amounts

⚠ Important:
Deposits and subscription charges are wallet transactions and count toward your monthly affordability limit.

🕹️ 4. Contest Refunds

Refunds are issued only in the scenarios below:

✅ 4.1 Contest does not launch

If a contest fails to begin (e.g., insufficient participation):

→ Full entry fee refunded to Deposit Wallet within 48 hours.

✅ 4.2 Verified technical failure

If PICK2WIN confirms system failure affecting contest participation:

→ Full refund within 48 hours.

💼 4.5 Refund Destination

All refunds go to the user's Deposit Wallet only, never to bank cards or accounts.

This ensures AML safety and compliance.

⚠️ 5. Ineligible Refund Scenarios

Refunds will not be issued for:

❌ Deposits correctly credited
 ❌ Contest entries for contests already started or completed
 ❌ Poor team performance, poor ranking or scoring dissatisfaction
 ❌ Device/app issues on user's side
❌ Internet or connectivity problems
 ❌ Bank/Payment-gateway delays
 ❌ Expired/free bonuses

❗ Important Dummy Teams & Line-Up Responsibility

Users may join contests early using dummy/manual placeholder teams.

If a user:

Joins contests early
Does not update teams after official line-ups
Plays with non-playing or out-of-XI players

👉 This is 100% the users responsibility.
👉 No refunds will be issued.

Reason:
PICK2WIN provides full editing before lock; failing to update is not a platform error.
